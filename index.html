Nestled within the resort grounds, Apung Oting's is the place to refuel after a swim or share a long, relaxed meal. Classic dishes like kare-kare, adobo, and sinigang, grilled favorites, and fresh-from-the-farm ingredients. Every plate carries the warmth of Pampanga's culinary heritage.
